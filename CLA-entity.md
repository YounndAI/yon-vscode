# YounndAI Entity Contributor License Agreement ("Entity CLA")

Thank you for your interest in contributing to YounndAI (the "Project") on
behalf of an organization. Where contributions are made by employees,
contractors, or agents of a legal entity, MARLINK TRADING SRL ("YounndAI",
"We") must have an Entity Contributor License Agreement ("Entity CLA") on file
that has been signed by an authorized representative of that entity ("You").

This agreement is for the protection of both the contributing entity and
YounndAI; it does not change Your rights to use Your own Contributions for any
other purpose. If you are contributing on your own behalf rather than for an
organization, sign the [Individual CLA](CLA.md) instead.

---

## 1. Definitions

"You" (or "Your") shall mean the legal entity identified in the signature block
below, including all affiliates that control, are controlled by, or are under
common control with that entity. "Control" means (i) the power, direct or
indirect, to cause the direction or management of such entity, whether by
contract or otherwise, (ii) ownership of fifty percent (50%) or more of the
outstanding shares, or (iii) beneficial ownership of such entity.

"Contribution" shall mean any work of authorship, including any modifications
or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally submitted by You to
YounndAI for inclusion in, or documentation of, any of the products owned or
managed by YounndAI (the "Work"). For the purposes of this definition,
"submitted" means any form of electronic, verbal, or written communication
sent to YounndAI or its representatives, including but not limited to
communication on electronic mailing lists, source code control systems, and
issue tracking systems that are managed by, or on behalf of, YounndAI for the
purpose of discussing and improving the Work, but excluding communication
that is conspicuously marked or otherwise designated in writing by You as
"Not a Contribution."

"Contribution Submitter" shall mean an employee, contractor, or other agent
of You whom You have authorized to make Contributions to YounndAI on Your
behalf, as identified in Schedule A.

## 4. Representations

You represent that:

**(a)** You are legally entitled to grant the above licenses, and the person
signing this Agreement is authorized to bind Your entity.

**(b)** Each of Your Contributions is the original creation of one of Your
Contribution Submitters (see Section 5 for submissions on behalf of third
parties), and each such Submitter is authorized to make Contributions on Your
behalf.

**(c)** Your Contribution submissions include complete details of any
third-party license or other restriction (including, but not limited to,
related patents and trademarks) of which You are aware and which are
associated with any part of Your Contributions.

**(d)** You will identify in Schedule A all employees, contractors, and agents
authorized to make Contributions on Your behalf, and You will keep Schedule A
current.

## 5. Third-Party Contributions

Should You wish to submit work that is not the original creation of one of Your
Contribution Submitters, You may submit it to YounndAI separately from any
Contribution, identifying the complete details of its source and of any license
or other restriction (including, but not limited to, related patents,
trademarks, and license agreements) of which You are aware, and conspicuously
marking the work as "Submitted on behalf of a third party: [named here]."

## 6. Notification

You agree to notify YounndAI of any facts or circumstances of which You become
aware that would make these representations inaccurate in any respect. You also
agree to update Schedule A whenever Your authorized Contribution Submitters
change.
